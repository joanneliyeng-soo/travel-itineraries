Yellowstone — Old Faithful Area National Park
Stamp station: Old Faithful Visitor Education Center — open daily late April–early November, approx. 8AM–8PM in summer.
Plan: Stamp at the VC, then walk the Upper Geyser Basin boardwalk and watch an Old Faithful eruption (~90 min cycle). Then drive to Grand Prismatic Spring overlook trail (~1 mi paved).

Stamp station: Fossil Butte NM Visitor Center · 864 Chicken Creek Road, Kemmerer, WY 83101

Reminders & To-Dos
✈
Book flights early. JAC has limited capacity and summer fares spike fast. Price out SLC as an alternate return — often cheaper with more flight options.
🏨
Book hotel as soon as dates confirm. West Yellowstone and Jackson sell out well in advance. Best Western properties in both towns. Budget $100–$200/night, expect high end in summer.
🚗
Book rental car early. Summer inventory at JAC and West Yellowstone is very limited.
🔍
Verify Flagg Ranch is open for your dates. JDR Parkway stamp is seasonal (late May–early Sept). If closed, Colter Bay VC is the backup stamp location.
⏰
Start early on Day 2. Old Faithful boardwalk, Grand Prismatic, and Hayden Valley are best before 9AM and increasingly painful by midday in July–August.
📵
Download offline maps before leaving the hotel each morning. Cell service is very limited inside both Yellowstone and Grand Teton.
🎟
America the Beautiful covers all entry fees at Yellowstone, Grand Teton, JDR Parkway, and Fossil Butte — no additional charges.
＋
Optional bonus stamp: Canyon Visitor Education Center has its own distinct Yellowstone stamp if routing through the Upper Loop on Day 2.
