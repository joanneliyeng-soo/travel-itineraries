~8:00 AM · ~45–60 min from hotel via US-89 north
Wupatki
National Monument
◈Stamp #1 · Visitor Center · Opens 9AM
Red sandstone pueblo ruins with sweeping Painted Desert backdrop. Leaving at 7AM gets you here right at open with no crowds. ~45 min.
🚗 ~14 miles south on the loop road
~10:00 AM · ~20 min south on loop road
Sunset Crater Volcano
National Monument
◈Stamp #2 · Visitor Center · Opens 9AM
Dramatic black cinder cone and lava fields visible from the road. One admission covers Sunset Crater + Wupatki. ~45 min.
🚗 ~35 min south on US-89, then I-40 east to Exit 204
~11:15 AM · I-40 Exit 204
Walnut Canyon
National Monument
◈Stamp #3 · Visitor Center · Open 9AM–5PM
Stunning canyon overlook views from the rim-level visitor center. ~45 min.
🍽️ Lunch in Flagstaff (~15 min west on I-40) · ~30 min
~1:30 PM · ~1 hr via I-17 south + AZ-89A west
Tuzigoot
National Monument
◈Stamp #4 · Visitor Center · Opens 8AM
Hilltop pueblo ruins with sweeping Verde River Valley views. ATB pass covers entry (also covers Montezuma Castle). ~45 min.
🚗 ~25 min via AZ-89A east + I-17 north to Exit 289
~2:45 PM · ~25 min
Montezuma Well
National Monument
◈Stamp #5 · Separate station · Same NPS unit as Montezuma Castle
Ancient limestone sinkhole fed by natural springs. Accessible overlook. ~30 min.
🚗 ~10 min south on I-17 to Exit 289
~3:30 PM · ~10 min south
Montezuma Castle
National Monument
◈Stamp #6 · Visitor Center · Open 8AM–5PM
Cliff dwelling viewed from the accessible paved pathway below. Last stop of the day — back to Flagstaff via I-17 north (~45 min). ~45 min here.

Petrified Forest

Sunday, Oct 11  ·  Painted Desert & Ancient Logs

◈ 3 Stamps
Open Day 2 Route in Google Maps
🏨 Aiden BW → Painted Desert VC → Hubbell Trading Post → Canyon de Chelly
⚠️ Hubbell Trading Post and Canyon de Chelly are stretch goals. Petrified Forest alone is a full day — include these only if you're making excellent time. Both are better saved for a dedicated Navajo Country trip.
🚗

Depart Flagstaff 7:30AM

~1 hr 45 min east on I-40  ·  Enter at north entrance (Exit 311)  ·  Morning light ideal for Painted Desert colors

~9:15 AM · Show ATB pass at gate
Painted Desert Visitor Center
Petrified Forest NP — North Entrance
◈Stamp #7 · Visitor Center · Opens 8AM
17-minute film on petrified wood. ~25 min, then 2 miles south to the Inn.
🚗 ~2 miles south on park road
~9:50 AM
Painted Desert Inn
National Historic Landmark
◈Stamp #8 · Opens 9AM–4PM
1940s Pueblo Revival with Hopi murals by Fred Kabotie. Incredible panorama from the terrace. ~25 min.
🚗 Drive the 28-mile park road south  ·  Blue Mesa, Agate Bridge, Crystal Forest pullouts (all car-based, ~1 hr)
~11:15 AM
Rainbow Forest Museum
Petrified Forest NP — South Entrance
◈Stamp #9 · Museum & Visitor Center · Opens 8AM
Fossil exhibits and gift shop. Exit south onto Hwy 180 toward Holbrook. ~30 min.
🍽️ Lunch in Holbrook (~19 miles via Hwy 180)  ·  ~30 min  ·  Then I-40 East to US-191 North
~1:30 PM · ~1 hr from Holbrook via I-40 E + US-191 N
Hubbell Trading Post NHS
⚡ Stretch Goal
◈Stamp +1 · Visitor Center · Open 8AM–5PM (Navajo DST)
Historic 1870s trading post with Navajo rug weaving demonstrations. Free entry. ~30 min. On Navajo Nation — clocks are 1 hr ahead of Flagstaff.
🚗 ~40 min north on US-191 to Chinle
~2:45 PM · ~40 min north
Canyon de Chelly NM
⚡ Stretch Goal
◈Stamp +2 · Welcome Center · Open 8AM–5PM (Navajo DST)
Get the stamp at the Welcome Center, then drive the South Rim overlooks from the car — Spider Rock and White House Ruin overlooks are unmissable. ~45 min.
🚗 ~2 hrs 30 min back to Flagstaff via US-191 S + I-40 W  ·  Arrive ~7:30PM
